How to read this report
Green cells are real, observed sign rates. The cohort has had enough time to fully bake at that window.
Yellow cells are estimated from the most recent real rate using historical ratios. Reasonably reliable (R² ≥ 0.90).
Red cells are speculative projections. High uncertainty. Use directionally, not precisely.
Click any rate cell to drill down into the underlying deals. Click the i icon on predicted cells to see the math.
\u25B6 Estimation methodology & live ratio stats
How predictions work: For immature cohorts, the dashboard estimates future sign rates by dividing the cohort's most recent real sign rate by a historical ratio. This ratio describes how much of the longer-window rate is typically captured by the shorter window. For example, if the 14-day rate historically captures 64% of the eventual 28-day rate, and a cohort's real 14-day rate is 8%, the estimated 28-day rate is 8% / 0.64 = 12.5%.

How ratios are computed (self-calibrating): Every time this tab renders, the dashboard groups all currently filtered deals into monthly cohorts (min 30 matched deals each). For each window pair (e.g. 14d to 90d), it selects only cohorts mature enough to have real data for both windows, computes each cohort's ratio (shorter-window rate / longer-window rate), then calculates the mean, standard deviation, coefficient of variation (CV), and R-squared across all eligible cohorts. This means the model automatically recalibrates when you load new data, change filters, or when more time passes and cohorts mature.

Confidence bands: The low estimate uses mean + 1 stdev (more conservative divisor), and the high estimate uses mean - 1 stdev (more aggressive divisor). This produces an approximate 68% confidence interval.

Classification: R-squared ≥ 0.90 = "Estimated" (yellow). R-squared < 0.90 = "Speculative" (red). R-squared measures how well the shorter-window rate explains the longer-window rate across historical cohorts.

Minimum thresholds: A window pair needs at least 5 eligible cohorts (each with 30+ matched deals) to produce an estimate. Below that, the cell shows "--".

Glossary
Cohort View
Shows leads created in the selected period and tracks their downstream performance
Activity View
Shows all events (matches, schedules, signings, closings, payments) that occurred in the selected period regardless of when leads were created
Matched
Lead was connected to at least one partner (agent or investor)
Referred
Lead was referred/introduced to a specific partner
Scheduled
An appointment or meeting was scheduled with a partner
Signed
A signed agreement was executed (either agent listing agreement or investor purchase agreement)
Closed
The transaction was completed
Revenue Collected
Cash payments received from closed deals. In Activity view, this counts payments received in the selected period. In Cohort view, it counts payments from deals created in the selected period.
Agent
Real estate agent pathway — leads matched with a realtor
Investor (DBN)
Direct Buyer Network pathway — leads matched with a cash buyer/investor
CTP
Connected to Partner — leads that were matched or referred to at least one partner type
Hybrid Sign Rate
Combined sign rate across both Agent and Investor funnels
Hybrid Sign Rate (CTP)
Sign rate using only CTP leads as the denominator
